Privacy Policy

Edvora E-Learning Platform

1. Introduction

Welcome to Edvora. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our e-learning services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name and email address
  • Account and login information
  • Usage data (courses viewed, progress, activity)
  • Device and browser information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our learning services
  • Manage user accounts
  • Personalize learning experiences
  • Communicate updates and important notices
  • Ensure platform security

4. Data Protection

Edvora implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze usage, and improve platform performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics or content delivery. These services are required to keep your data confidential.

7. Your Rights

  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

Edvora reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.
